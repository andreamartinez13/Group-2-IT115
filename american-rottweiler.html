The American Rottweiler

The main difference between the American Rottweiler and German/ Roman Rottweilers is their origin. Besides that, American Rottweilers are generally a bit leaner and sleeker comapared to the more muscular european Rottweilers.
They dont differ much in their overall size but moreso in the way they carry their weight.
Their noses tend to be a bit smaller while their heads are longer with more space between the ears. Additionally, the American Rottweiler has an arched neck of medium length, a broad chest, and a well sprung oval ribcage noticeably narrower than the German Rottweiler.
The American Rottweiler shares the deep loyalty, high intelligence, and easy trainability as the German. They are every bit as able to be a fantastic family dog and are good with kids as their German relatives.
